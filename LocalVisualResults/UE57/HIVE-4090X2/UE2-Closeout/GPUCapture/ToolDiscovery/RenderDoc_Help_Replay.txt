usage: renderdoccmd replay [options ...] <capture.rdc>

options:
  -w, --width          The preview window width. (optional uint=1280)
  -h, --height         The preview window height. (optional uint=720)
  -l, --loops          How many times to loop the replay, or 0 for indefinite. (optional uint=0)
      --remote-host    Instead of replaying locally, replay on this host over the network. (optional string)
  -?, --help           print this message
